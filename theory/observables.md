## Observational Dictionary

Redshift:
1 + z = a_0 / a(t)

Hubble parameter:
H(z) from solution of modified Friedmann system

Comoving distance:
χ(z) = ∫_0^z dz'/H(z')

Luminosity distance:
d_L(z) = (1+z) χ(z)

Angular diameter distance:
d_A(z) = χ(z)/(1+z)

Observational constraints act on (H0, Ωm0, Ωr0, ΩΛ0, α, β).
