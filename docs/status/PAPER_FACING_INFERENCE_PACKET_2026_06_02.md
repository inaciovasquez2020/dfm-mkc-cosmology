# DFM-MKC Cosmology — Paper-Facing Inference Packet

## Status

`PAPER_FACING_PACKET_READY`

## Allowed Claim Form

The DFM-MKC cosmology repository provides a reproducible implementation and real-data anchored executable check surface for conditional cosmological inference.

## Required Paper Sections

1. Model definition.
2. Data provenance.
3. Likelihood and prior specification.
4. Baseline comparison.
5. Degeneracy analysis.
6. Identifiability limitations.
7. Reproducibility instructions.
8. Boundary and non-overclaim statement.

## Forbidden Claim Forms

- DFM-MKC proves Lambda-CDM false.
- DFM-MKC resolves dark energy.
- DFM-MKC resolves dark matter.
- DFM-MKC gives unconditional cosmological closure.
- Repository closure alone establishes physical correctness.
- Repository closure alone establishes parameter identifiability.

## Submission-Ready Boundary

The paper may present a reproducible conditional inference program.

The paper may not present unconditional cosmological closure without external validation and identifiability guarantees.
