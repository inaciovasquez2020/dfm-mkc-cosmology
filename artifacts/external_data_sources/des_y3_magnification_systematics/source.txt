DES Y3 Magnification Modeling and Systematics Source

Source:
Dark Energy Survey Year 3 results: Magnification modeling and impact on cosmological constraints from galaxy clustering and galaxy-galaxy lensing
J. Elvin-Poole, N. MacCrann, et al.
DES Collaboration
29 May 2023

Required role:
Weak-lensing / galaxy-clustering systematics source for DFM-vs-Lambda-CDM holdout testing.

Data/systematics layer supplied:
- DES Y3 galaxy clustering w(theta)
- DES Y3 galaxy-galaxy lensing gamma_t(theta)
- DES Y3 2x2pt / 3x2pt observable context
- MagLim lens sample
- redMaGiC lens sample
- DES Y3 footprint and mask assumptions
- source-galaxy sample assumptions
- lens redshift distributions
- source redshift distributions
- magnification coefficients C_sample
- Balrog magnification calibration
- Buzzard validation simulations
- MICE validation simulations
- covariance / nuisance-systematics context
- scale-cut and modeling context

Boundary:
Data/systematics source only.
Does not supply DFM equations.
Does not supply DFM action functional.
Does not supply DFM prediction map.
Does not execute DES likelihood.
Does not prove DFM.
Does not disprove Lambda-CDM.
Does not replace CDM.
